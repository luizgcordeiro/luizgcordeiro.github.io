Research

My main topics of interest are:
  • Groupoids, semigroups, and more generally semigroupoids;
  • Actions, partial actions, preactions, etc. of semigroupoids;
  • Algebras (C*, W*, Steinberg…) associated to semigroupoids
  • Stone like dualities; Banach–Stone like theorems
